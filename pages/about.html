About Us
Bosnian & Herzegovinian Basketball League

The Bosnian and Herzegovinian Basketball League is the top-tier professional basketball competition in Bosnia and Herzegovina. Known for its competitive and dynamic play, the league features prominent teams from various regions of the country.


With a structure that includes regular-season matches and playoffs, the league serves as a vital platform for local talent development and showcases high-quality basketball.
